Our Mission

Men of God exists to foster honest conversations that help men lead their families with strength, humility, and responsibility. We believe strong families build strong communities—and a strong nation—when men submit themselves to God's truth.

Finally, brothers, whatever is true, whatever is honorable, whatever is just, whatever is pure, whatever is lovely, whatever is commendable, if there is any excellence, if there is anything worthy of praise, think about these things.

Philippians 4:8 (ESV)
