Our Mission

Men of God exists to equip men to lead with strength, humility, and purpose.
We believe God calls men to:

  • Lead their families
  • Serve their communities
  • Stand firm in truth
  • Grow in faith
  • Encourage one another

This is a place for men who want to rise to that calling.

Honest conversations between Christian men - fathers, husbands, and patriots - growing as leaders under God for their families and communities.

Men of God exists to foster honest conversations that help men lead their families with strength, humility, and responsibility. We believe strong families build strong communities—and a strong nation—when men submit themselves to God’s truth.
