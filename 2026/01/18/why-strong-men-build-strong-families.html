Why Strong Men Build Strong Families

January 18, 2026

Biblical leadership starts at home. Here’s why it matters more than ever.

Strong families don’t happen by accident. Scripture places the responsibility of leadership squarely on men — not as tyrants, but as servants.

When men lead with humility, courage, and faith, families flourish. This leadership starts at home long before it ever reaches the church or the nation.

“But as for me and my house, we will serve the Lord.” — Joshua 24:15

This is why the mission of Men of God begins with equipping men to lead their families well.
